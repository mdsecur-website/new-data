Who is M & D Security Services

M & D Security Services is a proudly South African security company that has been protecting lives, property, and businesses since 2008. The company is officially registered with PSIRA and operates with a strong commitment to professionalism, discipline, and integrity in the security industry.

Our team consists of highly trained security professionals with backgrounds in law enforcement and specialized security operations. We pride ourselves on our strict commitment to maintaining the highest standards in the industry.

What sets us apart is our dedicated team of highly trained professionals, modern security technology, and a client-first approach. At M & D Security Services, we believe that your safety is our priority, a commitment we proudly uphold every day to give our clients peace of mind and complete protection.
